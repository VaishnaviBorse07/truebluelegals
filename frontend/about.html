Who We Are
India's Trusted Legal Counsel

The firm has been actively practicing in litigation undertaken in the Bombay High Court and Bombay City Civil Court at Mumbai and Goregaon and any other ancillary courts and tribunals in Mumbai. The firm has offices in downtown Mumbai at Fort, Andheri, Kandivali, and Borivali.

We are a multi-service Indian Law Firm with attorneys sharing decades of practice, knowledge and experience in the chosen practice areas of the firm. We have believed, preached and put in practice our values that has helped us face the challenges and create a niche for ourselves.

TBL is committed to excellence in the quality of its work and meeting client expectations — delivering service, experience and results in equal measure.
