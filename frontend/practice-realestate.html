Real Estate

Real estate transactions in India involve multiple layers of documentation, regulatory compliance and due diligence. True Blue Legals has a dedicated real estate practice that assists buyers, sellers, developers, landlords and tenants across all aspects of property law.

We have deep familiarity with the Mumbai property market and its regulatory environment — from RERA compliance to MCGM approvals and the complexities of redevelopment projects under SRA and MHADA schemes.

Our Real Estate Services Include

  • Title Due Diligence & Property Verification
  • Sale & Purchase Agreements
  • Development Agreements
  • RERA Compliance & Registrations
  • Lease Agreements (Commercial & Residential)
  • Joint Development Agreements
  • Leave & Licence Agreements
  • Housing Society Formation & Disputes
  • Mortgage & Property Finance Documentation
  • Redevelopment — SRA & MHADA Projects
  • Tenancy Disputes & Eviction Proceedings
  • Real Estate Litigation

To discuss how True Blue Legals can assist you with real estate matters, please contact us today.
